Complete Journey Itinerary

Day 1
BHOPAL TO DELHI - The Journey Begins â–¼
04:45 AM - BHOPAL BYE BYE
Gather up at Rani Kamlapati Station, Bhopal. Let's kickstart the journey with lots of laughter and vibes!! 🚂✨
06:00 PM - HELLO DELHI
Hop on our super comfy Urbania/Traveller as we begin our overnight road trip to Jibhi with fun games, activities and full shifoyage vibes.
  • Meet the Shifoyage team and your fellow co-travelers
  • Participate in a unique "Evolve Circle" ice-breaker session to spark connections
  • Reflective journaling activity: "What do I wish to evolve in me through this journey?"
  • Begin your overnight journey to the mountains with music, soft conversations, and under-the-sky games that plant the seeds of connection
Day 2
Arrival & Grounding in Nature â–¼
06:00 AM - Check-in & Freshen Up
Time to check in at our cozy hotel and freshen up. Let's step out to explore the magical vibes of Jibhi with Shifoyage special activities of course.
11:30 AM - Jibhi Waterfall & Mini Thailand
Arrive in the serene valley of Jibhi, nestled amid cedar and pine forests. Enjoy a warm welcome followed by Grounding Meditation and breathwork to tune into the energy of the place.
  • Visit the enchanting Jibhi Waterfall to soak in the calmness
  • Explore Mini Thailand (if time permits)
06:30 PM - Forest Flow Session
  • Forest Flow Session: A mindful nature walk where you'll engage in light movement, silence, and nature-based reflections
  • Journal Prompt: "What am I noticing in nature that reflects me?"

💛 Evening Spiritual Session

This night's Shifoyage session will be a spiritual immersion into unlearning false concepts and remembering the truth. Through reflective circles, soul-touching questions, movement, and shared silence - we will open doorways to:

  • A deeper understanding of the stories we've believed about relationships
  • A release of the pain that was never ours to carry
  • A reconnection to what real, conscious connection feels like

A gentle spiritual surgery - to heal the inner child's longing to be seen, loved, and accepted.

Day 3
🏔 Serolsar Trek + Soulful Dialogues â–¼
Early Morning - Sunrise Movement Session
Start your day with a refreshing Sunrise Movement Session - Yoga, stretches, and a connection circle.
Drive to Jalori Pass | Trek to Serolsar Lake
After breakfast, drive to Jalori Pass, located at 10,800 ft above sea level. Begin your trek to Serolsar Lake with part of the trail in mindful silence.

🌊 At Serolsar Lake:

  • Soak in the serenity and pay homage at the sacred Budhi Nagin Temple
  • "Still Lake, Still Mind" - Meditation session by the lake
  • Deep Group Discussion: Exploring select verses from the Gita + heartfelt talk on "What really matters in life today?"
  • Create a Collective Nature Mandala using natural elements, symbolizing our individual uniqueness within unity
Evening - Tribe Tapestry Session
Return to our stay and freshen up.
  • Share stories and jam on musical instruments
  • Sing meaningful songs and groove to the rhythm of togetherness
  • Dinner and cozy storytelling
  • Gratitude journaling
Day 4
🌿 Letting Go & Gathering Strength â–¼

🕉️ Early Morning - Adhyatmic Exploration of Life

As the first light of dawn touches the peaks, we gather for one of the most sacred sessions - an early morning dialogue with the soul.

We dive deep into the timeless question: "Why do we suffer?"

From a spiritual lens, we explore the root of suffering - not as pain, but as disconnection from our true nature. This session is about seeing life clearly and understanding suffering as a teacher pointing towards inner freedom.

Post Breakfast - Journey to Tirthan Valley
Begin with gentle morning meditation and tea session. Check out and head towards Gushaini village by the pristine Tirthan river.
Chhoie Waterfall - Release Ritual
Short trek to Chhoie Waterfall for powerful reflection activities:
  • "Mirror Walk": Each participant walks silently and reflects on what they're letting go of
  • Release Ritual: Write something you'd like to release on biodegradable paper and let the waterfall wash it away
Post Lunch - Departure
Depart for Aut to board our vehicle back to Delhi.
  • Bus Circle: Closing gratitude circle + sharing personal takeaways
  • Travel back with soft music, meaningful conversations, and lots of hugs
Day 5
🌆 Return & Rebirth â–¼
Early Morning - Delhi Arrival
Arrive in Delhi early morning.
  • Goodbye hugs and exchange of soul notes or letters (optional)
  • Leave with an open heart, meaningful memories, and a Shifoyage tribe forever in your corner

✨ Trip Ends, Transformation Continues

You return not just with photos, but with a renewed sense of self, deeper connections, and memories that will breathe life into your soul for years to come.
